Hi there! I'm Ling-Hao CHEN (陈凌灏 in Chinese, Evan in English)! I am now a Ph.D. candidate at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Heung-Yeung Shum (沈向洋 教授). I obtained my bachelor's degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Xidian University in 2022. My research interests lie in Character Animation, Digital Generation, Embodied Intelligence, and Machine Learning. I am a research intern at the International Digital Economy Academy (IDEA), working closely with Prof. Lei Zhang (张磊 教授).
